Are you ready to relax?

You can use this exercise as a help to calm yourself during stressful situation or to just learn how to relax your mind and body in simple steps.
Box breathing uses four simple steps. While doing it, it may be helpful to visualize a box with four equal sides.

1: Breathe in through the nose for a count of 4.

2: Hold breath for a count of 4.

3: Breath out for a count of 4.

4: Hold breath for a count of 4.

Repeat

You can read more about Box Breathing technique here: Norelli, S. K., Long, A., & Krepps, J. M. (2018). Relaxation techniques.
While there are many different forms of deep breathing exercises, box breathing can be particularly helpful with relaxation. Box breathing is a breathing exercise to assist patients with stress management and can be implemented before, during, and/or after stressful experiences.
