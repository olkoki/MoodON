Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Instruction:

Step 1: Sit or lie down comfortably. Try to avoid any distractions. Start the video if you want. With each step, concentrate on the release of tension.

Step 2: Starting at the feet, curl the toes under and tense the muscles in the foot. Hold for 5 seconds, then slowly release for 10 seconds.

Step3: Tense the muscles in the lower legs. Hold for 5 seconds, release for 10 seconds.

Step 4: Tense the muscles in the hips and buttocks. Hold for 5 seconds, release for 10 seconds.

Step 5: Tense the muscles in the stomach and chest. Hold for 5 seconds, release for 10 seconds.

Step 6: Tense the muscles in the shoulders. Hold for 5 seconds, release for 10 seconds.

Step 7: Tense the muscles in the face (e.g., squeezing eyes shut). Hold for 5 seconds, release for 10 seconds.

Step 8: Tense the muscles in the hand, creating a fist. Hold for 5 seconds, release for 10 seconds.

Be careful not to tense to the point of physical pain, and be mindful to take slow, deep breaths throughout the exercise.
