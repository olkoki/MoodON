BOX breathing

You can use this exercise as a help to calm yourself during stressful situation or to just learn how to relax your mind and body in simple steps.

Box breathing uses four simple steps. While doing it, it may be helpful to visualize a box with four equal sides.

1: Breathe in through the nose for a count of 4.

2: Hold breath for a count of 4.

3: Breath out for a count of 4.

4: Hold breath for a count of 4.

Repeat

Video is a visual aid. It plays in a loop, so you can try to relax as long as you need.
